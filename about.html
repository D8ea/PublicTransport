About Nepal Express Bus Service

Who We Are

Nepal Express Bus Service is a trusted and reliable transportation provider serving customers across Nepal. We are dedicated to offering top-quality bus travel experiences, ensuring comfort, safety, and affordability for all our passengers. Our fleet of modern buses is equipped with amenities that make your journey pleasant, whether you are traveling short or long distances.

Why Choose Us?

  • Comfortable, air-conditioned buses
  • Affordable fares and discounts
  • Reliable and punctual service
  • Experienced and friendly staff

Where We Operate

Nepal Express Bus Service operates across a wide network of cities and towns in Nepal. Our routes cover major cities and tourist destinations, ensuring easy access to some of the most beautiful parts of the country.

  • Kathmandu
  • Pokhara
  • Lumbini
  • Chitwan
  • Biratnagar
  • Butwal
  • Dharan
