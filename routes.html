Our Routes

Kathmandu → Pokhara

Duration: 6 hours

Pokhara is known for its tranquil lakes and breathtaking views of the Himalayas, making it a top destination for nature lovers.

Kathmandu → Butwal

Duration: 7 hours

Butwal is a bustling city with rich history and culture, serving as a gateway to western Nepal.

Kathmandu → Dharan

Duration: 8 hours

Dharan is a picturesque town at the foothills of the Mahabharat range, blending modern amenities with natural beauty.

Pokhara → Chitwan

Duration: 5 hours

Chitwan is home to Chitwan National Park, a UNESCO World Heritage Site renowned for its wildlife safaris.

Kathmandu → Biratnagar

Duration: 10 hours

Biratnagar is an industrial hub and the second-largest city in Nepal, offering a glimpse into the country's economic backbone.

Butwal → Nepalgunj

Duration: 6 hours

Nepalgunj is a vibrant city in the Terai region, known for its proximity to the Bardia National Park.

Kathmandu → Lumbini

Duration: 8 hours

Lumbini is the birthplace of Lord Buddha and a spiritual center with a rich cultural heritage.
